Kaanda Bhindi Sookha

Description

A dry bhindi recipe by Jaggu dada.

Ingredients

  • 1 onion
  • 200 gms ladies fingers
  • 1 garlic clove

Steps

  1. Chop onions into cubes and soak them in water.
  2. Wash and chop bhindi into cubes.
  3. Heat oil in a non stick pan.
  4. Add onions and bindi and keep it covered for 15-20mins
  5. Add 1 garlic clove and salt to taste.
