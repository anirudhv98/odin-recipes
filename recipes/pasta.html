Pasta

Description

Pasta is a beloved Italian dish made from durum wheat noodles, typically served with a variety of sauces like tomato, cream, or pesto. It's comforting, easy to make, and endlessly customizable. A perfect go-to for a quick and satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • 200g (7 oz) dried pasta (e.g., penne, spaghetti, fusilli)
  • Salt
  • 1–2 tbsp olive oil or butter
  • 2–3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes or 1 cup cream (depending on sauce)

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta – Cook pasta in salted boiling water until al dente; drain and set aside.
  2. Prepare the sauce – Sauté garlic (and onions, if using) in olive oil, add tomatoes or cream, and simmer.
  3. Combine – Add the cooked pasta to the sauce and toss to coat evenly.
  4. Serve – Garnish with grated Parmesan, herbs, or a drizzle of olive oil.
