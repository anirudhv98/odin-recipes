Omelette

Description

An omelette is a fluffy, folded egg dish often filled with cheese, vegetables, or meats. It’s quick to prepare and perfect for breakfast or a light meal. Simple yet versatile, it can be customized with your favorite ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 2–3 large eggs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp milk or water
  • 1 tbsp butter or oil
  • Fillings of choice (e.g., cheese, onions, tomatoes, bell peppers, mushrooms, ham)

Steps

  1. Beat the eggs – Whisk eggs with salt, pepper, and a splash of milk or water.
  2. Prepare the pan – Heat butter or oil in a non-stick skillet over medium heat.
  3. Cook the eggs – Pour in the eggs and gently stir until just set, then let it cook flat.
  4. Add fillings and fold – Add your fillings to one side, fold the omelette in half, and cook for another minute until done.
