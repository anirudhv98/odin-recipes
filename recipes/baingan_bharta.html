Baingan Bharta

Description

an authentic and appetising dish by combining roasted eggplant with a specific combination of garlic, lemon, onions and tomatoes.

Ingredients

  • Large brinjals 5-6
  • Garlic cloves 15-16
  • Green chillies 5-6
  • Mustard oil 1 tbsp
  • Curry leaves 4-5

Steps

  1. Wash the brinjals well and wipe them with a clean kitchen towel.
  2. Make cuts on each brinjal from left to right.
  3. Add one green chilli in each of them, along with 2-3 garlic buds.
  4. Roast the brinjals on a low flame.
  5. Brush them with oil while roasting. Keep at it till they’re brown on all sides.
  6. After they’ve cooled down, squeeze off the burnt skin and mash the roasted brinjal, chillies and garlic in a bowl.
  7. Add salt to taste, along with curry leaves and a little mustard oil. Enjoy with a hot chapati!
