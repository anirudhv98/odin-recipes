Anda Kaddi Patta

Description

Made with egg whites, curry leaves and green chili, this egg recipe is a steal. Believe me, it gets ready in no time and could make up for a soothing and wholesome meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 egg whites
  • Handful of curry leaves
  • 1 green chili

Steps

  1. Take the eggs and break them into a bowl. Carefully, separate the egg yolks and put them into another bowl.
  2. Whisk the whites a little. Meanwhile, heat a pan and add oil.
  3. Add green chili and curry leaves when the oil is hot enough. Let it splutter.
  4. Now, add the curry leaves tempering into the egg whites bowl and mix.
  5. Thereafter, heat another non-stick pan and pour the egg white mixture on to it.
  6. Sprinkle salt and black pepper on the egg fry, as per taste.
  7. Cook from both sides and serve hot.
