Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, rich meat or vegetable sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese. It's baked to perfection for a hearty, comforting meal. Each bite offers a delicious blend of textures and savory flavors.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb (450g) ground beef or Italian sausage
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2–3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp dried oregano

Steps

  1. Prepare the sauces – Cook a meat or vegetable tomato sauce and a creamy béchamel (or use ricotta mixture).
  2. Boil or soak lasagna sheets – Use pre-cooked or softened pasta sheets as the base layers.
  3. Layer the lasagna – In a baking dish, alternate layers of sauce, pasta, and cheese.
  4. Bake – Cook in a preheated oven at 375°F (190°C) for 30–45 minutes until golden and bubbly.
